About me

Chris Wong, M.Mus Classical Guitar Performance (Yale), an award winning guitarist, studied with David Russel and Benjamin Verdery. He performs with various world class musicians, for example, violinist Takako Nishizaki and cellolist Wang Jian. Currently, he lives in Hong Kong. He is an adjunct lecturer and a classical guitar tutor/instructor in BUHK, HKU and CUHK resepctively.

Chris commits to promote musical education and culture. He was interviewed by different media, for example, HKATV-Culture Style, a TV programme of music culture apprication, where he performed for promoting classical guitar culture. Apart from his excellent guitar repertoire, his teaching achievement is also extarordinary. His students have won numerous international guitar competition awards. Three of his students were awarded the LTCL Advanced Guitar Practice Performance at the Trinity College of Music in London, UK at 90/100, 91/100 and 98/100. Other students are studying and graduating from prestigious music schools abroad, such as the Trinity College of Music in the United Kingdom and the Manhattan Conservatory of Music.
